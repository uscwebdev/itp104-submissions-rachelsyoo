Final Project Proposal

1. Topic

My website will be a personal statement project where I get to compile all of my creative works. I also wish to express myself and my creativity using the websit as an outlet.

2. Audience

My audience will be everyone who resonates with my creative vision. I will be compiling all of my works - from my illustrations to my works of literature. I hope to use this project as a portfolio where friends, family, and possibly my future employers can keep up with what I'm doing.

3. Design and Style

A design style that I have been very interested in lately is the Brutalist Movement, where a lot of different elements come together to form one piece. One thing I like about this art style is that the longer you look at a piece, the more interesting and meaningful it becomes. I plan to format my webpage very untraditionally, will the navbar and menu put in very unconventional places.

Some Colors I am planning on incorporating are black, blue, and possible purple. I want black to be the primary color and all other colors to be used as accent colors.


Some websites I used as inspiration for my design are:

  1. Pull and Bear
  2. Ganni
  3. The Roger

  1. Home Page

  3. Resume
